Beverly hills
90210

Notably one of the most famous neighborhoods in the U.S., Beverly Hills is a neighborhood where even the street signs indicate that you’re somewhere special.

Located in Los Angeles County, California, Beverly Hills plays home to movie and television stars both on the screen and off. It boasts some of the most extravagant shopping, dining and hotel destinations in the world, including the infamous roads Rodeo Drive and Wilshire Boulevard. Rare for L.A., the area features mostly wide, flat lots that provide ample space to build homes that bring fantasy-like amenities to life. Many celebrities, athletes and entertainment professionals make their home here, where luxury is a lifestyle and dreams become realities. Beverly Hills residents are proud to live in a place where exclusivity, privacy and impeccable taste are all served as a first class experience.
