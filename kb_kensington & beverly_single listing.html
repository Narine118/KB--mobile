about the property

Sited on the most prestigious cul-de-sac in the exclusive Bird Streets, one finds a daring architectural with panoramic views.

Designed by Paul McClean and furnished by Minotti, striking contemporary features dramatic 13ft ceilings and automated disappearing walls of glass that open to grassy yard and infinity edge pool.
