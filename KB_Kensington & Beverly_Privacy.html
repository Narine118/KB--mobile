Collection of your Personal Information 

Kensington & Beverly may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name. If you purchase Elite Luxury Homes’s products and services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete the purchase transaction. Kensington & Beverly may also collect anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your age. We may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Elite Luxury Homes. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Kensington & Beverly website.

Kensington & Beverly encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Kensington & Beverly so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Kensington & Beverly is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Kensington & Beverly website.

Use of your Personal Information

Kensington & Beverly collects and uses your personal information to operate its website(s) and deliver the services you have requested.

Kensington & Beverly may also use your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Kensington & Beverly and its affiliates. Kensington & Beverly may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Kensington & Beverly does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

Kensington & Beverly may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Elite Luxury Homes, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Kensington & Beverly may keep track of the websites and pages our users visit within Elite Luxury Homes, in order to determine what Kensington & Beverly services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Kensington & Beverly to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Kensington & Beverly will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Kensington & Beverly or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Elite Luxury Homes; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Elite Luxury Homes, or the public.
